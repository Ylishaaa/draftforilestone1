DESCRIPTION

The goal of Amaze-ing Kyle is to complete a series of challenging mazes while avoiding unexpected jumpscares

HOW TO PLAY

Move like a maze master! Use the W, A, S, D keys or your trusty arrow keys to navigate through the twists and turns. Feeling overwhelmed by the spooky vibes? Smash the spacebar to pause and catch your breath. If things get creepy and overwhelming hit ESC to exit the game
